Hello there! This is Anup.
I am a highly motivated individual pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics at the University of Minnesota's Carlson School of Management. I have developed a strong foundation in technical skills and a passion for leveraging data to drive business insights and decision-making.

With a strong aptitude for technical skills and a deep-rooted passion for data-driven decision-making, I find immense satisfaction in dissecting and analyzing large datasets, constructing predictive models, and providing valuable recommendations.

Throughout my professional journey, I have had the privilege of leading teams and gaining valuable experience in diverse industries such as technology, insurance, education, and agribusiness. However, my ultimate goal has always been centered around extracting meaningful insights from data and leveraging them to deliver significant value to organizations. I have had the opportunity to engage in notable projects including developing product recommenders, conducting root cause analysis, performing impact analysis, and implementing customer segmentation strategies.

Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I find solace and joy in the great outdoors. Exploring new hiking trails has become one of my most cherished and dedicated hobbies, allowing me to rejuvenate and embrace the wonders of nature.

Overall, I am driven by my ambition to excel in the field of business analytics, leveraging my technical expertise and passion for data to make a positive impact.
